Our product, Robobuddy

Robobuddy is designed to help children between the age of 8-12 suffering from sleep paralysis. With our technology we have we created a helpful robot linked with a heart rate monitor hidden inside a bracelet. Going to sleep with this bracelet on will make sure your paralysis will be picked up by RoboBuddy. We introduce you the functions that RoboBuddy is equipped with:

Your heartbeat will be monitored during the night. When the bracelet senses an abnormal heartbeat it will send links to RoboBuddy. He will help you to calm your mind and stop the attack.

The (pre-)recorded voices will guide you through the paralysis by talking to you. Even before and after the attack we will make sure you can go to sleep without worrying by talking to you and going through precautions.

When Robobuddy is activated and an attack occurs, he will switch all the lights on. He will make sure the scary dark corners are forever gone. You will recognize a safe space.

RoboBuddy can play your favorite music to calm you down. Let robo choose a song or make your own selection! It's all possible. Make your attack less scary by playing happy or soothing music. Robobuddy is the best DJ you can ask for!
